1) Problem & Goals

Core issue: Citizens struggle to find reliable, current info for public services. AI can help—but only if users trust it.

  • Clarify when to rely on AI vs. official pages or agents.
  • Show how answers were derived (sources, timestamps).
  • Communicate uncertainty with confidence bands and safe defaults.
  • Respect privacy with explicit consent and retention choices.
Safe to act Verify with official site

Design Principles

  • Explainable: citations, reasoning summary, confidence.
  • Controllable: strict mode (official sources only), opt-out of retention.
  • Accountable: disclaimers, quick human escalation.
  • Inclusive: FR/EN, plain language, WCAG-conscious.
